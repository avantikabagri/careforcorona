About us

Scrolling through Facebook in late March of 2019, I saw a GoFundMe post my friend had shared. One of my favourite restaurants on campus needed to raise funds to survive the COVID-19 pandemic.

Realizing that there must be several other local campus business in a similar situation, Care for Corona was born. A centralized website that seamlessly allows people to support local campus businesses.
